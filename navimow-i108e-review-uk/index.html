Quick verdict

The i108E is a good mower in an awkward spot. It extends the proven i105E platform to 800m² — but at the official UK price of £1,299, it's undercut by Navimow's own i208 AWD, which covers the same 800m² for £899 with all-wheel drive on top. Buy the i108E only if you specifically want the most mature i1-series platform, or if a promotion drags its price under the i2 models.

  • Buyers who value the i1 platform's longer real-world track record over the newer i2 hardware.
  • Anyone who catches it on promotion below ~£900 — at that price the calculus flips.

FAQs

What's the difference between the Navimow i105E and i108E?

Coverage: 500m² vs 800m² on the same i1-series RTK + vision platform. At current official prices (£699 vs £1,299) the coverage step costs £600.

Is the i108E better than the i208 AWD?

On paper, no — the i208 AWD covers the same 800m² for £899 and adds all-wheel drive. The i108E's case is the longer i1-platform track record, which some buyers reasonably value.
