The wired alternatives

If a one-afternoon boundary-wire install doesn't put you off, the Flymo EasiLife GO 250 (~£424, 250m²) and Gardena Sileno Minimo 500 (~£429, 500m²) remain quiet, proven Husqvarna Group machines at prices wire-free can't yet match. For heavily shaded gardens, gardens over 2,000m², or serious slopes where you want a manufacturer-confirmed rating, the dealer-backed Husqvarna range is the one to look at:

£1,199 RRP (dealer prices vary)

Best for: Heavily shaded or complex small gardens where satellite mowers struggle, and buyers who value a national dealer network.
