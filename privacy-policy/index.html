Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-07-11

AutoLawn UK ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why, in plain English, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics 4 (loaded via Google Tag Manager) to see which pages are read and how the site performs. These are optional analytics cookies. We use Google Consent Mode v2 with analytics and advertising storage set to denied by default, so no analytics cookies are set until you click "Accept analytics" on our cookie banner. If you accept, Google may process usage data (including a truncated/approximate IP and a randomly generated identifier) to produce aggregated reports; we never see individual visitors' identities. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time via the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.
  • Newsletter: if you subscribe, we store your email address with our email provider solely to send the newsletter. Unsubscribe links are in every email.
  • Contact: if you email us, we keep the correspondence only as long as needed to reply.

Cookies and consent

Strictly necessary cookies (and equivalent storage) that make the site work are always active — this includes remembering your cookie choice itself. Optional analytics cookies are only set after you accept them on our banner, in line with UK PECR and UK GDPR. Rejecting them leaves the site fully functional; you simply won't be counted in our analytics. To review or change your decision, use "Cookie settings" in the footer.

Advertising

We do not currently run display advertising, and no advertising cookies are set. Consent Mode already defaults advertising storage to denied. If we introduce advertising in future (for example Google AdSense), it will be added to the same consent banner and this policy will be updated to name the partners involved before any advertising cookies are set.

Affiliate links and third parties

When you click an affiliate link, the destination retailer (e.g. Amazon UK, Segway Navimow) may set cookies to attribute the sale. Those cookies are governed by the retailer's own privacy policy — we never see your personal purchase details, only anonymous, aggregated commission reports.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us. You can complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you're unhappy with our response.

Controller

AutoLawn UK is operated by an independent UK publisher. For any data request, contact us via the contact page and we'll respond within one calendar month as UK GDPR requires.
