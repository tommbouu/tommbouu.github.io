Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-07-04

AutoLawn UK ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why, in plain English, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

  • Analytics: we may use analytics (such as Google Analytics 4 or a privacy-respecting alternative) to see which pages are read. Where consent is required for analytics cookies, a banner will ask before any are set. This policy will name the specific tool once one is in use.
  • Newsletter: if you subscribe, we store your email address with our email provider solely to send the newsletter. Unsubscribe links are in every email.
  • Contact: if you email us, we keep the correspondence only as long as needed to reply.

Advertising

We do not currently run display advertising. If we introduce it in future (for example Google AdSense), advertising partners may set cookies to show and measure ads; a consent banner compliant with UK/EEA requirements will ask before any advertising cookies are set, and this policy will be updated to name the partners involved.

Affiliate links and third parties

When you click an affiliate link, the destination retailer (e.g. Amazon UK, Segway Navimow, Mammotion) may set cookies to attribute the sale. Those cookies are governed by the retailer's own privacy policy — we never see your personal purchase details, only anonymous, aggregated commission reports.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us. You can complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you're unhappy with our response.

Controller

AutoLawn UK is operated by an independent UK publisher. For any data request, contact us via the contact page and we'll respond within one calendar month as UK GDPR requires.
