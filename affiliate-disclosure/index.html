What affiliate links are

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and then buy something, the retailer or manufacturer pays us a small percentage of the sale. You pay exactly the same price either way.

We participate in (or are applying to) affiliate programmes including: Segway Navimow (via Awin), Amazon Associates UK (as an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases), other Awin merchants, eBay Partner Network, and Impact merchants. The current list may change; this page is updated when it does.

What it doesn't change

  • Rankings and recommendations are decided by fit for your garden — size, slope, budget — before monetisation is considered.
  • We recommend products with no affiliate programme when they're the right answer (and mark them as such).
  • Negative findings from professional reviewers are included even when they hurt conversion — see, for example, our coverage of wet-grass traction complaints.

How you can tell

Commercial pages carry a disclosure box at the top. Product buttons are affiliate links unless labelled otherwise.
