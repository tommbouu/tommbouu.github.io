FAQs

Is the Luba Mini AWD worth £300 more than the Navimow i105E?

Only if you have slopes or rough ground. On a flat lawn the i105E does the same job for ~£699. On a bank or terraced garden, the Luba's AWD is worth every pound of the difference.

Which is easier to set up, Luba or Navimow?

Both are app-guided wire-free setups measured in minutes-to-an-hour, not days. Both need RTK antenna placement with decent sky view — neither has a meaningful edge for a typical garden.

Can either mower handle multiple lawn zones?

Yes, both brands support multi-zone mapping in their apps. Check the specific model's zone count and transfer behaviour (can it drive between zones?) against your garden layout before ordering.
