Slope explainer: % vs degrees

Manufacturers quote slope as a percentage: rise ÷ run × 100. A 35% slope ≈ 19°; 80% ≈ 38.7°. Measure yours with a spirit-level app on a plank, then check the model's rating — and keep a safety margin, especially for wet grass. Full detail in the slopes guide.
